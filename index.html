About Me

Philosophy Statement: My personal goal as a dental hygienist is to influence my community towards a healthier and happier lifestyle that begins with motivational oral health. I am passionate about this profession since my influence was a very caring and compassionate dentist I had when I was growing up. I always felt comfortable in a dental chair, hence my desire to transmit the same sentiment to others. When I graduate, I plan on dedicating time at volunteer facilities that provide free dental service to the community. The professional impact that dental hygiene has in me is profound because I truly believe that an individual's well-being can improve by having a better oral health. Providing assistance during the client's journey is a personal joy that I like to bring.

The purpose of the Risk Assessment Project is to allow the beginning dental hygiene student to integrate knowledge obtained from didactic courses into the assessment, planning and implementation, and re-evaluation of a comprehensive preventive treatment for a patient. This project allows the student to incorporate the following aspects of dental hygiene services:


  1. Assessment of current oral hygiene and periodontal status in relation to the patient’s past and present oral and health history.
  2. Evaluation of the need for fluoride, or other products, in caries control and disease prevention.
  3. Assessment of nutritional needs as they relate to the patient’s oral health.
  4. Identification of possible cultural/ethnic aspects that may influence the development or progression of periodontal disease.
  5. Develop a proper dental hygiene treatment plan.
  6. Implement the dental hygiene treatment plan.
  7. Evaluate the outcome of oral hygiene instruction, nutrition, and CAMBRA assessment.

The purpose of the Comprehensive Care Project is to allow the senior student to integrate knowledge obtained from didactic courses into the assessment, planning and implementation of patient treatment. This project allows the student to incorporate the following aspects of dental hygiene services:


  1. Assessment of current oral hygiene and periodontal status in relation to the patient’s past and present oral and medical history.
  2. Evaluation of the need for fluoride in caries control and disease prevention.
  3. Assessment of nutritional needs as they relate to the patient’s oral health.
  4. Periodontal scaling and root planing
  5. Placement of antimicrobial or antibiotic medicaments
  6. Determination of periodontal maintenance
  7. Dental referrals
